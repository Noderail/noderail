Early Access

Access is not a gate. It is a handshake.

NodeRail is in early access. Tell us about your work and Gao Kabubi will review your request personally. The process takes less than five minutes.

Submit a request

We will respond within 48 hours.

Be specific. This helps us understand how NodeRail can best support your work.

What happens next

We review your request

Gao Kabubi reviews every request personally. We respond within 48 hours.

A brief conversation

For most requests, we schedule a 20-minute call to understand your work and help you structure your first node.

You publish your first node

We walk you through the node template and help you publish your first structured, citable piece of intellectual work.

Your DOI is issued

On your first stable release, Zenodo issues a permanent DOI. Your work is now citable in academic contexts.

Who gets access first

→ Researchers building new fields or sub-fields
→ Students with capstone or dissertation work ready to publish
→ Practitioners with frameworks they want to make citable
→ Universities or research centres with cohort-level research programmes
→ Anyone who has already identified a specific intellectual continuity problem in their work
