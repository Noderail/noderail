Node types

Four structured units of intellectual work

Field

Field Node

Defines a domain: scope, constructs, ethics, governance, and a constitution for contributions.

Framework

Framework Node

A reusable method, model, or operational playbook inside a field. Citable and forkable.

Measurement

Measurement Node

An instrument, index, or scale for measuring a construct. Versioned and linked to its field.

Project

Project Node

A capstone, case study, or pilot with a required Continuation Hook so work compounds.

Founder Field

The first field being built on NodeRail

Founder Field  ·  DRAFT

Human Capacity Science (HCS)

HCS is the measurement and design of cognitive, emotional, and digital load so capacity does not silently degrade into burnout, errors, or disengagement. The first field to be built on NodeRail, with a complete Field Constitution, Construct Map, Measurement Philosophy, and Ethics Charter in development. Founded by Gao Kabubi, also founder of Dustoff Reset, a cognitive health tool built on HCS principles.
